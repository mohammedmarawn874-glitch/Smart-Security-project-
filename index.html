Summer project

My Summer of Solutions project focuses on creating an AI-driven smart security systemdesigned to improve safety in public and private spaces. The idea is to move beyond traditional CCTV monitoring by using real-time computer vision to detect unusual behavior, suspiciousmovements, and potential threats automatically The system can analyze video streams instantly, highlight activities that need attention, andsend alerts to security staff without any manual monitoring. It also uses basic predictive analytics to identify patterns that may lead to incidents, helping security teams respondfaster and prevent problems before they escalate. The solution is meant to be simple, scalable, and suitable for places like malls, schools, residential areas, or workplaces. It reduces the pressure on human guards, increases responsespeed, and provides a more reliable layer of protection. Overall, the project aims to make security smarter, more proactive, and easier to manageusingAI.

Problem Statement (Smart Security):

Traditional security systems rely heavily on human monitoring, which makes it difficult todetect threats, unusual behavior, or incidents in real time. Security staff cannot track multiple camerasefficiently, leading to delayed responses and missed risks. This results in unsafe environmentsin public spaces, malls, schools, and residential areas. There is a clear need for a smarter, faster, and more reliable security approach.

How can I solve the problem

We install cameras throughout the city to monitor everything that happens in the city

What are the benefits of artificial intelligence?

It analyzes all cases, such as theft, kidnapping, fire, fainting, and inappropriate behavior, andsends a message detailing the case, its location, and its nature. It also captures images of theincident, identifies the individuals involved, and searches for them in the database to facilitatethe search for the person in cases of theft, kidnapping, or any other crime

Impact on society

This will reduce crime, speed up rescue operations, make the work of police and firefighterseasier, and increase the sense of security
